Privacy Policy

Data Protection

At Auramax AI, your privacy is our priority. We do not sell or share your data with anyone, including other users. Your data is strictly used within the Auramax AI platform to enhance your experience and provide personalized results.

If you wish to remove your data from our systems, we fully comply with deletion requests, ensuring all account and related information is permanently removed.

Data Security

We implement strong security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Industry-Standard Authentication: Ensuring only authorized users can access the app.
  • Regular Security Audits: Conducting periodic checks to ensure our platform remains secure.
  • Employee Access Controls: Only authorized personnel have access to sensitive data.

Your personal data is stored on Microsoft Azure, which adheres to global data protection standards.

Face Data Collection and Use

We collect face data in the form of selfies. Here’s what you need to know:

  • What Face Data Does the App Collect? We collect one selfie per user, per day. These selfies are analyzed using proprietary AI models to examine facial features and generate your personalized results.
  • Planned Uses of Collected Face Data The collected selfies are exclusively used for analysis within the Auramax AI app. No data is shared with third parties, and no other users can view your face data. The analysis is used solely to improve your experience by providing personalized recommendations and reports.
  • Where Is the Data Stored? All face data is stored securely on Microsoft Azure servers, which implement strict encryption and protection measures.
  • Will Face Data Be Shared with Any Third Parties? No. We do not share, sell, or transfer your face data to any third party under any circumstances.
  • How Long Will Face Data Be Retained? We retain face data until either:
    • You request account deletion, at which point all your data is permanently removed from our systems.
    • After 30 days of an inactive subscription, at which point your face data is automatically deleted.

Limitation of Liability

While we take all possible precautions to protect your data, we cannot be held liable for events beyond our control, including but not limited to:

  • Unauthorized access to your personal devices.
  • Data breaches on third-party platforms.
  • Any information you voluntarily choose to share with others.

Your Rights

You have the following rights concerning your data:

  • Access: You may request access to all personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: If there are inaccuracies in your data, you may request corrections.
  • Deletion: You may request the deletion of your personal data at any time.
  • Opt-out: You may opt out of specific data processing activities that you do not wish to participate in.
